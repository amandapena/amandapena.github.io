Hi, I'm Amanda

Welcome to my home on the internet. Thanks for stopping by.

I'm a digital campaigning strategist currently based in San Diego, CA. Since 2018, I've worked at ACRONYM, building winning digital campaigns and digital infrastructure to serve the progressive movement.

I've been working in the digital fundraising and advocacy sphere for six years. Before ACRONYM, I led digital campaigning and supporter engagement at Oxfam America. There, I worked across all campaigning issues, from our highly successful #RefugeesWelcome campaign to build public support for refugees, to our massive push to #FightFamine in four countries on the bring of famine. Before I worked at Oxfam, I was a Client Manager at M+R where I worked for a bunch of amazing clients on digital fundraising and advocacy including Planned Parenthood, Union of Concerned Scientists, Pathfinder International, and Bread for the World.
